Game-Rules

  1. Pay attention to the sequence of colors that are flashed.
  2. After each round, repeat the sequence by clicking the colors in the same order.
  3. Start with a single color flash.
  4. If you succeed, the game will add another color in the next round.
  5. Keep following the sequence and clicking the colors correctly.
  6. The sequence will grow by one color after each successful round.
  7. You'll need to remember and click the entire sequence in order.
  8. If you make a mistake or take too long, the game ends.
  9. How far can you go? Can you reach 10 steps?
